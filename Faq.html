Freaquently Asked Questions (FAQ)


1. How do I place an order?

Browse our collection, select the item, choose the size and quantity, and click "Add to Cart." Proceed to checkout, provide shipping details, and complete payment. You'll receive an order confirmation email.


2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment options.


3. What are your shipping options?

We offer standard and express shipping options. Delivery times and availability will be shown during checkout.


4. Can I track my order?

Yes, we provide a tracking number and link once your order is shipped


5. What is your return policy?

Contact us within 7 days if you receive a damaged or incorrect item. For more details, visit our Returns & Exchanges page.


6. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Additional customs duties and taxes may apply.


7.How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us through our Contact Us page or by emailing support@kosmo.com.

8. Do you have a size guide?

Yes, we provide a size guide on each product page for accurate sizing information.
